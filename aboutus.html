About Us


Just Goodness Sweet Creations

Once upon a time there was flour and sugar....
What began as a hobby in 2019 has gradually blossomed into what is now known and loved as Just Goodness Sweet Creations. At Just Goodness, our aim is to provide the best quality macarons, cakes and other delectable desserts using only the finest, top tier ingredients. Our french macarons are especially known far and wide Trinidad and Tobago as the best.
We have had the privilege of providing our sweet treats for various celebrations such as birthdays, anniversaries, milestones, weddings and baby showers. At Just Goodness, you are guaranteed that each item is made with love. As we continue to grow, we are humbled by and are extremly thankful for the unwavering support all the way!

Here's to sweet adventures together!
