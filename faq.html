The French macaron (pronouced ma-kuh-ruhn) is a sweet meringue based confection made up of egg whites, icing sugar and almond flour. Considered a sofisticated and delicate dessert, macarons have a crispy shell with a soft, chewy interior. These gluten-free confections are often filled with chocolate ganache, jams or buttercream

French macarons are made with almond flour therefore they are classified as a gluten free item.
